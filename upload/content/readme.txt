
Content
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

This is the main content folder for all your groupfolders and
imagefolders which ultimately contain all your images.

Here you may create, delete or rename folders as you like, to create
the desired structure of your imagevue gallery. In general, the
structure looks like this:

content/groupfolder/imagefolder/(images)
