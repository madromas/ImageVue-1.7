
LOOPS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

This folder contains a handful of audioloop segments. They may be used
if you are setting the audioplayer audiostyle=loop, and you may
ofcourse use your own mp3 loops. You may also set an optional folder
for your loops in the configuration file(config.ini).
