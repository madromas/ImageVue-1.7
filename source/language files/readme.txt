
LANGUAGE FILES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
You can change the language/text in your image gallery by editing 
lang.ini and saving it in your main imageVue folder.

You may also give it a new name, like french.ini, save it in your 
imagevue folder and refer it through your configfile: lang = french.ini.

We may provide additional languages as we recieve them.
